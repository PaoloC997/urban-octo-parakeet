Frequently asked questions

Do I need to book in advance?

We recommend it, especially on weekends, holidays, and during high season in spring and summer. Still, we always help walk-ins whenever we have availability.

Can I rent for several days?

Yes. We offer attractive discounts for rentals of 2, 3, or more days.

Can I get route recommendations?

Of course. We love sharing our favorite routes and local tips so you can discover Valencia like a local.

What type of bikes do you have?

We have comfortable, well-maintained city bikes in different sizes, ideal for adults and families, including options for children.

Are helmet, lock, and accessories included?

Yes. We provide a helmet, lock, and basket or bag as needed, at no extra cost.

What happens if it rains or the weather is bad?

No problem. You can cancel or modify your booking at no cost if the weather does not cooperate, according to conditions.

Is it safe to cycle in Valencia?

Yes. The city has a good network of bike lanes and many quiet areas. We will point you toward the safest and most pleasant routes.
