About Ono

A neighborhood workshop for better rides

Ono Bikeshop is a bike rental and workshop in Ciutat Vella, València. We help riders keep their bikes moving with clear advice, careful repairs, premium maintenance, custom builds, and restoration work.

Built around the way you ride

Every bike comes in with a different story. Some need a fast safety check before a city ride, some need a full seasonal service, and some are ready to become something more personal. We look at the details, explain the options, and recommend work that makes sense for the bike.

The workshop covers cleaning, maintenance, adjustments, replacements, lubrication, custom builds, and detailing. The goal is simple: reliable bikes, clean results, and service that is easy to understand.
